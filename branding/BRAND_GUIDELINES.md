## Voice & Tone

### Marketing Copy
- **Tone:** Confident, helpful, straightforward
- **Style:** Short sentences, active voice, benefit-focused
- **Example:** "Less time on Canvas, more time teaching"

### Product Interface
- **Tone:** Clear, encouraging, efficient
- **Style:** Action-oriented, concise, friendly
- **Example:** "Building your course..." not "Please wait while we process..."

### Error Messages
- **Tone:** Helpful, not blaming, solution-oriented
- **Style:** Explain what happened + what to do next
- **Example:** "Canvas token expired. Generate a new one in Canvas Settings ‚Üí Approved Integrations"

## File Formats

### Logo Files
- **SVG:** Primary format (scalable, crisp at any size)
- **PNG:** For platforms that don't support SVG

- **Social media:** PNG (platform-specific sizes)
- **App icons:** PNG (iOS/Android required sizes)
